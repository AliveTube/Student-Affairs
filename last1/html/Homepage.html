Our departments

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is concerned with the development of systems that exhibit behavior typically associated with human cognition, such as perceiving, learning, communicating, reasoning, making decisions, and acting in a physical and social environment.

Information Technology

One of the biggest drivers of a successful business is efficiency, and the ability to automate routine tasks is a great way to increase overall efficiency. Generally speaking, the IT department is responsible for providing the infrastructure for this automation.

Information Systems

Information systems attract their inputs of data, information and knowledge, store and process them, and extend their outputs and deliver them to employees and decision makers in the organization.

Decision Support

The Decision Support department is typically responsible for providing analytical and strategic support to decision makers within an organization.
